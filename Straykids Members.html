Straykids

Straykids is one of K-pop's greatest 4th generation boy group.
They debuted on the 25th of March, 2018.
They have had many successful comebacks and have recieved a lot of love from their fans.


Straykids Members

Bang Chan

Bang Chan is the leader of Straykids. He is also the oldest of the group. His Korean name is 방찬 and his stage name is Bang Chan.
He is also one of the producers of Straykids. The producing group is called 3racha.
Bang Chan was born in Australia but moved to Korea to train.


Lee Know

Lee Know is the second oldest of the group. He has the role of main dancer.
His Korean name is 이민호 and his real name is Minho Lee.
Lee Know is in the unit Danceracha, which is Straykids' dance unit.


Changbin

Changbin is the third oldest of the group. He is the main rapper and also the sub-vocalist.
His Korean name is 서창빈 and his real name is Changbin Seo.
He is also in 3racha and helps produce music for the group. Many people have refered to him as the fastest rapper in K-pop.


Hyunjin

Hyunjin is the fourth oldest of the group. He is the lead dancer and the visual of the group.
He went on a haitus for three long months because of serious accusations, but came back during Thunderous promotions.
He has posted several dance cover videos; some of which he choregraphed himself.
He has shown that he not only has a good looking face but that he is talented in many different ways.
His Korean name is 황현진 and his real name is Hyunjin Hwang. He is also in Danceracha.


Han

Han's real name is Han Jisung and his Korean name is 한지성.
He is also known as the all-rounder of the group. The reason is because he can dance, sing, rap, and produce music.
He is in 3racha as well and he has also written and posted many of his own songs on Youtube.


Felix

Felix's Korean name is 이용복 and he is the lead dancer and rapper of the group.
His deep, charismatic voice has earned him a lot of respect and admiration.
He was born in Australia and lived there for some time before coming to Korea to become a trainee.
Because of his extended time in Australia, he has an accent when speaking English. Many people love his accent and say that it is one of his attractive points.
He is in Danceracha and has proved his dancing skills time and time again.


Seungmin

Seungmin is the main vocalist of the group.
His Korean name is 김승민 and he was born in Korea. He is known for his angelic voice as well as his innocent facial features.
He is in the unit known as Vocalracha.


I.N.

I.N., otherwise known as Jeongin Yang, is the maknae and vocalist of the group.
His Korean name is 양정인 and his fans call him 아기빵 (Baby bread) because of his cute face.
He is also in Vocalracha and many praise him for his unique voice
